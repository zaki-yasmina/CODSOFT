Scientific Research Supporting Our Formulas

Our commitment to your pet's health is backed by scientific research. We continuously study the nutritional needs of pets to develop the best diets possible.

Some key studies include:

  • Study on Protein Sources: Research shows that high-quality protein is essential for muscle maintenance in pets.
  • Grain vs. Grain-Free Diets: Studies indicate that whole grains can be beneficial for digestive health.
  • Omega Fatty Acids: Research highlights the importance of Omega-3 and Omega-6 fatty acids for skin and coat health.

We regularly update our formulations based on the latest research to ensure that your pet receives optimal nutrition.
